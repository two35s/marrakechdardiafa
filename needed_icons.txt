LIST OF ICONS NEEDED FOR MARRAKECH DAR DIAFA WEBSITE
===================================================

GENERAL NAVIGATION & UI
-----------------------
- Logo Icon: A modern house or abstract circular architectural shape.
- Home: A simple house icon.
- Catalogue: A grid or dashboard layout icon.
- Map: A location pin or map marker icon.
- Admin: A settings gear or user-shield icon.
- Search: A magnifying glass icon.
- Filters: An adjustments or horizontal sliders icon.
- Chevron Down/Up: Small arrows for dropdowns and accordions.
- Arrow Left/Right: Navigation arrows for the image gallery.
- Back Arrow: A return arrow for navigating to previous pages.
- Heart (Outline & Filled): For "Like" and "Saved" properties.
- Share: A three-dot or arrow-out icon for sharing listings.
- Plus: An "Add New" icon for the Admin Dashboard.
- Edit/Pencil: To modify existing listings.
- Trash/Delete: To remove listings from the database.
- Close/X: To dismiss modals and mobile menus.

PROPERTY FEATURES (AMENITIES)
----------------------------
- Rooms/Bedroom: A bed or layout icon.
- Area/Size: A ruler, square meter (m2) symbol, or grid icon.
- Floor: A stacked layers or stairs icon.
- Price: A currency tag or simple clock icon (as currently used).
- Wi-Fi: Standard wireless signal bars.
- Air Conditioning: A snowflake or fan icon.
- Balcony: A balcony railing or outdoor view icon.
- Dishwasher: A dish or appliance icon.
- Elevator: Up/Down arrows inside a box.
- Furnished: A sofa or armchair icon.
- Parking: A "P" inside a square or a car icon.
- Washing Machine: A laundry or tumble dryer icon.
- Heating: A radiator or flame icon.
- Terrace: A sun or parasol icon.
- Smart Home: A house with a circuit or lightning bolt.
- Concierge: A service bell or person icon.
- Gym: A dumbbell or weight icon.
- Swimming Pool: Water waves or a pool icon.

SOCIAL MEDIA & EXTERNAL
-----------------------
- Instagram: Standard app camera logo.
- Facebook: Simple "f" logo.
- Twitter/X: Simple X or bird icon.
- WhatsApp: Bubble with phone handset.
- Email: Envelope or "at" (@) symbol.
- Phone: Handset or mobile phone icon.

MOBILE SPECIFIC
---------------
- Menu (Hamburger): Three horizontal lines.
- Close (X): To exit the mobile menu.
- Plus (Menu Toggle): A thin plus (+) sign that rotates to an X.


STATUS BADGES
-------------
- Available: A green checkmark or "tick" icon.
- Verified: A star or badge with a tick.
- Realtor/Premium: A building or house-shield icon.
- New Listing: A "New" spark or bolt icon.
- Featured: A crown or trophy icon.

MAP SPECIFIC
-----------
- Map Marker: A custom pin with a price or house icon.
- List Toggle: A list-bullet icon.
- Layout Toggle: A grid/window icon.
